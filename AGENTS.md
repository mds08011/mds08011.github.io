# Project: Personal Website

## General Instructions
*   When generating code, prioritize **semantic HTML5** over older practices.
*   Ensure all new features are accessible and follow [WCAG guidelines](https://www.w3.org) (e.g., proper ARIA labels, color contrast ratios of at least 4.5:1).

## Testing Instructions
